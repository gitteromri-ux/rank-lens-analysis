Executive Summary

Top reps convert better. The bottleneck is how many of them we put on the phone.

Across 122 weeks of Hebrew-Related and Biblical-Related calling, top-tier reps (rank 4) convert contacted leads into SOV at 1.4–1.6× the rate of rank-1 reps. But total weekly SOV is essentially uncorrelated with the rank-3–4 share of contacts — when that share rises, it's because more top reps showed up (Spearman ρ between r34 share and r34 reps active = +0.44), not because bottom reps were displaced. The previous "negative response curve" was a mix-shift artifact in the per-contact rate. The real lever is utilization: only 15–23% of the available top-rep roster is active in any given week.

Methodology

How the productivity & utilization story was built

01

Causal rank join

Ranks are published every Sunday from each rep's prior 10 weeks of registrations within their (school × language) pool. For every daily row we attach the rank that was known on that date — the rank set on the most recent prior Sunday. No forward-looking leak.

02

SOV definition

Daily rep-level SOV is ActualStatCRMCourseSOV + UpsalesAmount. Rolled up to weekly totals (the primary metric) and to a contacted-weighted per-contact rate (a diagnostic).

03

Per-rank productivity

For each rank tier we sum SOV and contacted leads across all rep-days, then divide. This is the rep-level productivity gradient — independent of who shows up which week.

04

Utilization

Roster size = distinct reps observed at rank 3 or 4 over the full window. Current active = average distinct r3–4 reps active per week in the most recent 4 weeks. Utilization = active ÷ roster.

05

Why total SOV, not the rate

Total weekly SOV directly reflects revenue generated. The contacted-weighted per-contact rate is a ratio, and ratios are vulnerable to mix-shift: when more top reps show up, total contacts rise faster than SOV's marginal lift on those contacts, pulling the rate down even as the business is winning more. We show both, but only the total drives the narrative.

06

Significance

Spearman ρ tested two-sided. We test r34 share vs total SOV (the headline), r34 share vs SOV/contact rate (the artifact), and r34 reps active vs total SOV (the lever). Categories with fewer than 10 weeks were excluded.
