Methodology

How this is computed

  • SOV definition. ActualStatCRMCourseSOV + UpsalesAmount per rep-week.
  • Rank source. RankRegFromCalls, joined per rep-week-language. Rank 1 = lowest, 4 = highest.
  • Cohorts. Hebrew Related and Biblical Related schools, split by rep LanguageName (English vs Spanish). Other languages excluded.
  • Active roster only. Reps with "disabled" in their name are flagged as ex-employees and excluded from rosters, productivity, utilization, and the staffing list — so every metric reflects the people who can actually be deployed next week.
  • Bench definition. A rep is "bench" if they were active in fewer than 50% of the last 8 weeks. The recommendation engine targets a 75% activation rate.
  • Expected lift per rep. (0.75 − recent_activation_rate) × avg_sov_when_active. Reps need ≥ 4 historical active weeks to be recommended.
  • Cohort lift. Sum of the top 10 priority reps' individual lifts.
  • Operating baseline. Trailing 4-week average through , week granularity.
